About Me

Building events & brands with intent

I'm Balbeer Kumar Saurav, a final-year B.Tech Computer Science student at the Institute of Technology & Management, Gwalior. What started as organising one inter-branch tournament grew into founding the ITM Sports Cell — a campus-wide sports culture that didn't exist before.

Alongside academics, I run a sports-focused creative & growth practice, designing brand visuals and planning promotions for gyms, academies and local sports businesses around Gwalior. I move comfortably between spreadsheets and storyboards — between coordinating fifty volunteers on event day and laying out a clean Canva poster the night before.

My approach is simple: plan thoroughly, design intentionally, and show up for execution day like it's the only thing that matters.
